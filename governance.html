Institutional Structure

Governance

The Foundation's governing structure, board composition, oversight mechanisms, and institutional accountability frameworks.

Structure

Governing Bodies

The Foundation is governed by a Board of Directors responsible for strategic oversight, a President holding executive authority, and a Secretary responsible for institutional records and compliance. Operational units are directed through a Director of Programmes.

⚑

Board of Directors

Strategic oversight, fiduciary responsibility, and institutional direction. The Board sets policy and approves major programmes and partnerships.

Contact: dir@hasse.foundation

◈

President

Executive leadership and external representation. The President is responsible for programme delivery, partnerships, and institutional communications.

Contact: pres@hasse.foundation

✦

Secretary

Institutional records, regulatory compliance, board proceedings, and official correspondence.

Contact: sec@hasse.foundation

Principles

Governance Principles

The Foundation is committed to the highest standards of institutional governance, applying to itself the anticipatory and transparent governance principles it advocates for external systems.

  • Independence from political and commercial interests
  • Transparency in financial management and programme delivery
  • Accountability to beneficiaries, partners, and the public
  • Rigorous ethical standards in research and field operations
  • Inclusive decision-making processes

Oversight & Accountability

The Foundation undergoes annual financial review and publishes summaries of its activities and use of resources. The Board meets quarterly to review programme performance and strategic direction.

An independent advisory council provides external perspective on research quality, programme design, and institutional direction.
