Working at the Foundation

What We Offer

The Foundation offers a rigorous and intellectually demanding environment for individuals committed to governance innovation. Staff work at the intersection of legal theory, ecological science, and institutional design — engaging with some of the most complex governance challenges of our time.

We are a small, highly collaborative institution. Every member of the team contributes meaningfully to the Foundation's mission and has the opportunity to shape its research direction and institutional development.

  • Engagement with frontier governance research
  • Direct contribution to the c-ECO Framework and TFP
  • Field research opportunities in the Amazon
  • Access to the Foundation's fellowship and partnership networks
  • Collaborative, mission-driven institutional culture
